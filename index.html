Dry food for the prevention of urolithiasis with poultry

Happy Cat Minkas Urinary Care with Poultry is a balanced complete food for adult cats. Thanks to its high-quality and easily digestible protein, as well as a reduced content of magnesium and phosphorus, it helps to reduce the pH level of the urine and thus contributes to the prevention of urinary tract diseases. Manufactured in Germany to strict quality standards.

  • Promoting urinary tract health
  • Decreased urine pH
  • Decreased levels of magnesium and phosphorus
  • Yucca Schidigera to reduce odor

Ingredients: Poultry protein** (27%), corn, corn meal, meat meal, poultry fat, fish meal, beet pulp* (sugar-free), lignocellulose, sunflower oil, apple pulp*, potassium chloride, potassium chloride oil, yucca schidigera* (0.04%); Marks: * - dried, ** - dried, partially hydrolyzed.

Additives: Vitamins/kg: vitamin A (3a672a) 15000 IU, vitamin D3 (3a671) 1250 IU, vitamin E (rac-alpha-tocopherol acetate 3a700) 75 mg, taurine (3a370) 1000 mg. Antioxidants: tocopherol extracts from vegetable oils 1b306(i). Trace elements/kg: iron (iron (II) sulfate; monohydrate 3b103) 130 mg, copper (copper (II) sulfate pentahydrate 3b405) 12 mg, zinc (zinc oxide 3b603) 100 mg, manganese (manganese 3b) 15 mg, iodine ( E2, anhydrous calcium iodate 3b202) 1.5 mg, selenium (E8; sodium selenite) 0.15 mg. Amino acids: DL-methionine (3c301) - 5,000 mg.

Analytical composition: Crude protein 32.0%, crude fat 12.0%, crude fiber 2.5%, crude ash 5.5%, calcium 1.1%, phosphorus 0.8%, sodium 0.4%, potassium 0.6%, magnesium 0.08%, omega-6 fatty acids 2.5%, omega-3 fatty acids 0.25%
